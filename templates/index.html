About Me

I'm driven by a passion for learning and making a difference in the field of cybersecurity. The ever-evolving cat-and-mouse nature of cybersecurity excites me, and I’m always looking for ways to stay ahead of emerging threats. I’m highly proficient in Python, C#, JavaScript, React Native, and Expo, with additional experience in C++, x86 Assembly, and Java.

I've developed numerous websites and applications, both for personal use and professional projects. Constantly working on new technologies keeps me motivated, whether it's for personal innovation or solving challenges for others.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance from Norwich University, as well as a Master of Engineering in Cybersecurity Policy and Compliance from George Washington University. Additionally, I have earned my Security+ certification. These accomplishments have given me a strong technical foundation and a hunger to continue learning in this ever-changing field.

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy spending time on the golf course, hanging out with friends, and working on my car, which I race in autocross. My passion for cars and engineering mirrors my love for solving complex problems—whether it’s fine-tuning an engine or building secure systems, I’m always focused on performance and precision.
